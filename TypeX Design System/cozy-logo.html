Cozy logo directions

Playful, warm, a little hand-made.

A warmer palette than the indigo tech-brand — cream paper, terracotta accent, soft shadows. The italic X stays the signature across every variant, but the voice shifts from "quiet editor" to "cozy writing nook." Each card shows the lockup, the mark by itself, and the lockup at a small size.

01Warm slab
Fraunces at a chunky weight with its playful italic X — a bookish, serif-y wordmark that feels like the cover of a beloved notebook. No separate mark needed.
